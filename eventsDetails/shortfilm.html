Short Film

Rules and Regulations

• It is an individual event and only 3 participants per institution are allowed

• The short film can be in any genre

• The duration of the short film should be of 5 to 10 minutes

• The short film should not contain any obscene, defamatory or other objectionable content

• The short film should have subtitles if they are made in a language other than Tamil and English

• The credits for various aspects of the film like direction, cinematography, sound editing (at least the four categories) should be included in the film text as well as on the CD/DVD along with other information like the title of the short film, duration etc…

• The submitted entries i.e. CD/DVD will not be returned.

• The short film must be submitted to the event coordinator 30 minutes before the commencement of the event.

• Decision of the judges will be final.
