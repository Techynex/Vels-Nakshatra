About us

Vels Nakshatra is an annual cultural extravaganza that illuminates the vibrant spirit and talent of the students at Vels University. It is a celebration of creativity, artistry, and unity, where students from diverse backgrounds come together to showcase their talents and participate in a myriad of cultural, artistic, and intellectual activities.

The event spans over several days and is filled with a plethora of competitions, performances, workshops, and exhibitions that cater to a wide range of interests and talents. From dance and music to art and literature, Vels Nakshatra offers a platform for students to express themselves, learn from each other, and celebrate their passion for the arts.

One of the highlights of Vels Nakshatra is its cultural competitions, where students compete in various categories such as dance, music, drama, and fashion. These competitions not only bring out the competitive spirit in students but also showcase the rich cultural heritage and diversity of India.

Apart from competitions, Vels Nakshatra also features a variety of performances by professional artists and performers, adding a touch of glamour and excitement to the event. These performances range from traditional folk dances to contemporary music concerts, providing entertainment for everyone.

In addition to the cultural events, Vels Nakshatra also hosts a series of workshops and seminars conducted by experts in various fields. These workshops cover a wide range of topics such as art, literature, film, and technology, providing students with valuable insights and knowledge.

Vels Nakshatra is not just about competitions and performances; it is also about fostering a sense of community and camaraderie among the students. It is a time for students to come together, make new friends, and create lasting memories.

Overall, Vels Nakshatra is a celebration of talent, creativity, and culture. It is a testament to the vibrant and dynamic student community at Vels University and a showcase of the immense potential and creativity of the youth of today.
